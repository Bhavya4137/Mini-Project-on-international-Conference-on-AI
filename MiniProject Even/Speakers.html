Featured Speakers

Peter Diamandis

Founder of the X PRIZE Foundation

Diamandis is the Founder & Executive Chairman of the XPRIZE Foundation, which leads the world in designing and operating large-scale incentive competitions. He is also the Executive Founder of Singularity University, a graduate-level Silicon Valley institution that counsels the world's leaders on exponentially growing technologies

Daniel Sieberg

VP of Public Relations, Huawei USA

He previously spent two years in entrepreneurship as a founder, co-founder, and strategic advisor focused on developing technologies like AI, AR, blockchain, news aggregation, and social media. He continues to serve as an advisor to Good Trust, which is building the future of how to manage your digital afterlife and also spent time working with nonprofits like First Street Foundation, which is building the first U.S. database of flood risk.

Colin Angle

Chairman, CEO, Founder, iRobot

Colin Angle is co-founder, chief executive officer and chairman of the board of iRobot Corporation, maker of the Roomba vacuum cleaning robot and the PackBot military robot. He holds a Bachelor of Science in electrical engineering and a Master of Science in computer science from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

Ron Galloway

Expert on the Disruptors' (Apple, Google, & Amazon) Impact on Healthcare

Ron Galloway is a researcher, public speaker, the director of 4 films, and the author of 3 books. Ron’s current focus is the intersection of Big Tech companies & Healthcare. This is the subject of his 60 minute presentation "The Disruptors." He has spoken at hundreds of conferences, is a graduate of Georgia Tech, and was a portfolio analyst for 20 years. His work has been written about in the New York Times, the Wall Street Journal, and New Yorker magazine. He has been featured on CNN, CNBC, the BBC, and Jon Stewart's Daily Show

Anindya Ghose

Artificial Intelligence Expert

The youngest recipient of the prestigious INFORMS ISS Distinguished Fellow Award, Ghose, and his work are consistently recognized. His research has been honored with 16 best paper awards and nominations; he is also the winner of the highly prestigious NSF CAREER award. Ghose has published more than 100 papers in premier scientific journals and conferences and has been awarded 14 research grants from Adobe, Google, Microsoft and several other corporations. Additionally, he is a frequent and revered speaker and has delivered more than 200 talks internationally.
